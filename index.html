Who I Am

Hello! I'm Lynette Shen, a passionate Product Designer with a love for creating delightful and functional user experiences. With a background in design thinking and a keen eye for aesthetics, I focus on blending creativity with intentional problem-solving to meet user needs.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring art, diving into UX research, or collaborating with like-minded individuals to bring ideas to life. My mission is to craft designs that resonate with people and make a positive impact on their lives.
